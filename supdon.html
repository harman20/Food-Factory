Super Donuts is a Chandigarh-based donut brand and coffee house chain founded by Ketan Kalra in 2012, with his first outlet in Chandigarh. In 2013, Super Donuts introduced bagels, wraps and shakes for health-conscious and sugar-free consumers. By 2015, the company had expanded its menu to include meals.
